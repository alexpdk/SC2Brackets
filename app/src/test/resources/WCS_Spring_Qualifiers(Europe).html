    Format[edit]

    The 2019 WCS Spring - Europe Qualifier allows 4 players to win travel, accommodation and entrance fully paid to 2019 WCS Spring.
    Format consists of an open qualifier, followed by a 16 players double-elimination group stage ending up in 8 players single-elimination final bracket.
    Players who qualified for GSL Season 1, will not be eligible to compete in Challenger Season 1.

    Open Qualifiers[edit]

    • Day 1
      • April 13, 2019 - 16:00 CEST
      • Double-elimination bracket.
      • Top 4 move into Groups Stage.
    • Day 2
      • April 14, 2019 - 16:00 CEST
      • Double-elimination bracket.
      • Top 4 move into Groups Stage.

    Groups Stage[edit]

    • From April 20, 2019 - 16:00 CEST to April 28, 2019 - 16:00 CEST
    • Double-elimination group stage.
      • All matches are Bo3.
      • Top 8 advance to the final Playoffs.

    Playoffs[edit]

    • May 04, 2019 - 14:00 CEST
    • Single-elimination playoffs.
      • All matches are Bo5, except for the Finals, which are Bo7.
    • Top 4 advance to the Main Event.
