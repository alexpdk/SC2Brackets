    Format[edit]

    • This tournament is open to CIS player of any skill level.
    • All matches are Bo3 until the final, which are Bo5.
    • The maps will be picked using the ABAB format used in WCS. The higher seed bans first (A). The second player bans (B). The bans continue until only three maps remain. The lower seeded player then picks the first map, and the higher seeded player picks the second map. (ABAB BAC)
